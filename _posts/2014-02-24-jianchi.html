Courage is resistance to fear, mastery of fear, not absence of fear.

勇气是抵御恐惧，把握恐惧，而不是没有恐惧。

Dreams are one of those things that keep you going and happy.

梦想，就是一种让你坚持，让你幸福的东西。

If wrinkles must be written upon our brows, the only thing you can do is not letting them be written upon the heart.

如果额头终将刻上皱纹，你只能做到，不让皱纹刻在你的心上。

Doing what you like is freedom, liking what you do is happiness.

自由是做你自己喜欢的事，而幸福却是喜欢你自己做的事。
